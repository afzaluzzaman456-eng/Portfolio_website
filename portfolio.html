Urban Riders

This is a modern ride-sharing and vehicle booking website. Users can log in via Google, Facebook, or GitHub to instantly book a ride, track available vehicles in real-time on the map, and select the perfect ride for their journey.

  • Seamless Booking: Simply enter your pickup location and destination to find available drivers nearby.
  • Live Map Tracking: View your current location and track regional vehicle availability instantly.
  • Flexible Travel: Choose from a variety of vehicle options tailored to your specific travel needs.

Urban Riders — Route & Vehicle Selection

Once you input your travel details, the platform calculates your route and presents the available vehicle options alongside a real-time tracking map.

  • Selected Route: An orange route summary panel confirms a trip from Mirpur 1 to Dhanmondi.
  • Vehicle Options: Multiple available cars are listed with transparent details, showing a standard capacity of 4 passengers at a flat rate of 100 taka per ride.
  • Live Driver Mapping: The integrated map displays live vehicle markers clustered near the Mirpur-1 starting point.
